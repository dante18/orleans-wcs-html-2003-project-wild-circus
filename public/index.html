Welcome to Wild Circus

Circus Wilde Circus was founded in 1890 by two brothers who were passionate about the circus world. They wanted to share their passion by offering unforgettable performances.


The first performance of the circus occurred in a small village near their home, this was their first success. 5 years later, they were performing all over the country. The two brothers decided to try a new adventure by performing in other countries. With time, their reputation grows.


After a few years, they performed all over the world by offering always beautiful and unforgettable shows to the delight of the spectators.
